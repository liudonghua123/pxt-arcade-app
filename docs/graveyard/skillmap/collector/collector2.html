    game.over(true)

Step 5

üéä Little celebrations üéä

We can easily add some flavor every time our sprite reaches a coin. All we need to do is expand the destroy block.

‚ñ∫ Click the ‚äï to the right of the ||sprites: destroy [otherSprite] ‚äï|| block (inside the on sprite overlaps container.)

‚ñ∫ Click the word spray to open a dropdown. Choose the effect that works best with your game. (For coins, try the rings effect!)

‚ñ∫ Change 500 ms to 200 ms to keep the effect short and sweet.

sprites.onOverlap(SpriteKind.Player, SpriteKind.Projectile, function (sprite, otherSprite) {
    //@highlight
    otherSprite.destroy(effects.rings, 200)
    info.changeScoreBy(1)
})
