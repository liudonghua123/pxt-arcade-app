Bunny Glow-Up

Introduction

Give the bunny a glow-up!

This quick tutorial will show you how to design your own character for an amazing bunny swap.

Step 2 - Play the OG

For a preview of what your swap might look like, open the game window and press the (A) and (B) buttons.

  • :mouse pointer: When you’re done playing, click Next to get to the next instruction.

Step 3 - Design the Hero Character

Make it your own!

  • :mouse pointer: Click on the white bunny called Glow-Bunny-Yours and choose Edit from below the Asset Preview pane.

  • :paint brush: Change the bunny in any way you want or delete the bunny altogether and draw something new.

  • :mouse pointer: When you’re happy, click Done to go back out to the My Assets window.

Step 4 - Play Again

Play your game again.

Test the game with your new art.

Don’t forget to press (A) and (B) to go back and forth between characters.

Step 5 - Design More

  • :paint brush: Want to change more? You can also play around with the Standard-Bunny and the sparkle background animation.

  • :mouse pointer: When you’re done, test your game again and see how you like it!

Step 6 - Finale

Change the art as many times as you want. Once you’re happy with your game, click Done to share your glow-up with friends!

namespace SpriteKind {
    export const Animation = SpriteKind.create()
}
controller.B.onEvent(ControllerButtonEvent.Pressed, function () {
    animation.stopAnimation(animation.AnimationTypes.All, myBg)
    myBg.setImage(assets.image`placeholder`)
    mySprite.setImage(assets.image`Standard-Bunny`)
})
controller.A.onEvent(ControllerButtonEvent.Pressed, function () {
    animation.runImageAnimation(
    myBg,
    assets.animation`sparkle`,
    300,
    true
    )
    mySprite.setImage(assets.image`Glow-Bunny-Yours`)
})
let myBg: Sprite = null
let mySprite: Sprite = null
scene.setBackgroundColor(13)
mySprite = sprites.create(assets.image`Standard-Bunny`, SpriteKind.Player)
myBg = sprites.create(assets.image`placeholder`, SpriteKind.Animation)
myBg.z = -50
