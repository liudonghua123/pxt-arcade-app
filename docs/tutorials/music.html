Make a Music Player

Introduction

Before technology hits the market, it goes through many changes.

Today, you’ll see what it might have been like to be on the prototype team for Zune back in 2005.

~hint What was a Zune? 💡


The Zune was a Microsoft branded portable media player that released in 2006. You could store music, videos, and pictures on it. It also had a radio feature.

Zune released three different versions between 2006 and 2011, and you can see the original version featured Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3.

hint~

~hint What is a prototype? 💡


A prototype is an early model of something that is about to be produced.

Prototypes don’t always work like the final product, but they allow inventors to see how ideas will work in the real world.

There are a few reasons you might not be able to hear your song even if the code is correct.

  • Try turning up the volume on your computer
