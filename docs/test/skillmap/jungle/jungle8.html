Make It Your Own

Welcome

Create your own levels!

In this activity, you‚Äôll edit images and tilemaps to create a custom game!

3. A New Location

Let‚Äôs change the scene
üñºÔ∏è üñºÔ∏è üñºÔ∏è

  • :paint brush: Click background in the My Assets window, then click Edit under the Asset Preview.

  • :paint brush: You can either customize the current background in the Image Editor or toggle to the Gallery to choose something new.

4. Edit Your Monkeys

üôà Change the Monkey üôà

  • :paint brush: Go through My Assets and edit each of the monkeys. You can choose new characters and animations from the Gallery or draw your own!

5. Try It!

üïπÔ∏è Play your game after each step

When you play your game, it‚Äôs easier to see what changes need to be made to your characters.

6. A New Level

üöß Let‚Äôs dig in to the level editor

  • :paint brush: Scroll down to find level1, then click Edit.

üí° You should see the full tilemap for level 1. You can zoom in or out using the magnifying glasses at the bottom.

7. Start Small

Let‚Äôs make the character‚Äôs starting platform longer.

  • :binoculars: Look for the single hovering tile at the lower left of the screen. To make it longer, click the skyblock square beneath My Tiles and make sure the pencil icon is selected above.

  • :paint brush: Click on either side of the floating platform to add extra skyblocks.

8. Build a Wall

Add walls on top of your new tiles so that your character doesn‚Äôt fall through.

  • :paint brush: Click the large wall icon (it has a corner missing at the top) and make sure the pencil is still selected.

  • :paint brush: Click on each new skyblock to add a wall to the tile (it should shade overtop with a light red color.) Then click Done.

9. Try It!

üïπÔ∏è Give it a try üïπÔ∏è

Is your game platform longer? Does it hold your main character?

10. Go Crazy!

Now that you know how to add tiles and walls, you can change the levels whichever way you like.

üí° Remember that your code is built to work with certain tile names only. If you add new tiles or change the names, you‚Äôll need to go back to the code to add your changes!

Finale

When you‚Äôre done playing your new game, click Done to return to the main page to share your final game, then open it with a full toolbox to edit even more!
